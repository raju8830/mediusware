Software Engineer (PHP)

Dhaka, Bangladesh

Job Context

We are looking for Software Engineer (PHP) who is an expert in PHP frameworks eg. Laravel and Javascript frameworks eg. VueJS/React JS/Angular to join our team.

Job Requirements:
  • Strict adherence to deadlines to meet the project requirements.
  • ICPC contestants will get preference.
  • Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.
  • Ability to listen to personnel, understand their needs, propose alternatives, and specify the best solution.
  • Capable to work under pressure.
  • Excellent time management and multitasking skills.
Job Responsibilities:
  • Extensive experience in software development, scripting and project management
  • Experience in PHP (Laravel ) must be needed
  • Familiarity with various PHP frameworks (e.g. Yii, Codeigniter, Symfony) will be plus point
  • In-depth knowledge of relational databases (e.g. MySQL, PostgreSQL) and NoSQL databases (e.g. MongoDB, Elasticsearch)
  • Must have knowledge at least in one among Vue CLI / React JS / Angular
  • Familiarity with various operating systems (Linux, Mac OS, Windows)
  • Ability to work independently
  • Ability to continue multiple projects in parallel
  • Collaborate with internal teams, Review and debug code and Perform validation and verification testing
  • Working knowledge of version controlling systems like Git, clear understanding of Gitflow workflow, repository management tools like BitBucket and understanding of overall CI/CD process.
Educational Requirements:

At least Completed Graduation in CSE. Having a professional degree in Laravel, and PHP will be added advantage
