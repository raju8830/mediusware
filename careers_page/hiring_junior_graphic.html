Hiring Junior Graphic Designer

Dhaka, Bangladesh

Job Context

We are looking for some creative graphic designers having expertise in post-production sectors with skills like Clipping Path, Neck Joint, Hair Masking, Liquify, and Retouch using Adobe Photoshop.

Job Requirements:
  • Proficiency with required desktop publishing tools, including Photoshop, InDesign Quark, and Illustrator.
  • Ability to work in a group and able to share innovative ideas.
  • Self-driven and have a positive attitude towards work
Job Responsibilities:
  • Need to be expert with Clipping path, Neck Joint, Retouching, Background Removing, Hair Masking, Color correction, High-end beauty retouch.
  • Preparing finished art by operating the necessary equipment and software.
  • Reviewing final layouts and suggesting improvements when necessary.
  • A strong eye for visual composition.
  • Ability to work under a tight time schedule.
Educational Requirements:

HSC, Diploma in Graphic Arts / Graduation from any Institute. Educational
qualification can be relaxed for experienced candidates.
