Hiring SEO Specialist

Dhaka, Bangladesh

Job Context

We are looking for SEO Specialist for our office in Dhaka. The SEO Specialist will be an expert to manage all search engine optimization and will be responsible for managing all SEO activities such as content strategy, link building and keyword strategy to increase rankings on all major search networks. You will also manage all SEM campaigns on multiple platforms in order to maximize ROI.

Job Requirements:
  • Proven 4+ of excellent knowledge across various data streams: search analytics (SEO/SEM), website analytics (Google Analytics & Adobe Analytics), Social (earned through social listening, owned paid and organic through native platforms or social media management platforms like Hootsuite, Sprinklr etc.) and demographics (GWI, Surveys, qualitative interviews, etc.)
  • Knowledge of ranking factors and search engine algorithms
  • Strong knowledge about popular SEO Tools (Google Keyword Planner, MOZ, SEMrush, Ahrefs etc.).
  • Knowledge and experience in any or several of the following tools: Excel, Tableau, PowerBi, Google Data Studio etc. with an advanced proficiency
  • Professional certification of Advanced Content and Social Tactics, Search Engine Optimization (SEO) is a plus.
  • Be a data ambassador, foster knowledge sharing across team members regionally as well as locally for more junior / interns
  • A self-starter, proactive, who identifies milestones and potential blockers and is able to solve them with minimum supervision
Job Responsibilities:
  • Develop strategy, and prioritize SEO recommendations for different international websites to deliver maximum business impacts
  • Collaborate with the project management and technology teams to support various SEO initiatives; drive early integration of SEO requirements into web development process
  • Audit clients' website content and/or user experience to find opportunities for growth in the organic search channel
  • Gather data from multiple platforms, including Google Analytics, Adobe Analytics; Google Search Console, Bing Webmaster Tools, and enterprise SEO tools.
  • Research keyword recommendations relying on a variety of factors and tools like Keywords planner and DMP
  • Utilize Excel spreadsheet and/or other BI/Visualization tools to compile client facing reports, studies, and presentations
  • Monitor the crawl and website SEO health to spot out any web crawl errors or any other areas that may have a negative impact on SEO and crawlability of the website
  • Analyzing, tracking and reporting on organic search performance, using data to report performance, trends, and progress on key initiatives
  • Be up-to-date with industry trends and applicable technologies, as well as developments in SEO, social media, and analytics; online marketing, e-commerce, and online consumer behavior
