UI/UX Designer

Dhaka, Bangladesh

Job Context

We are looking for UI/UX Expert for our office in Dhaka.

Job Requirements:
  • 2+ years of working experience UI & UX Designer or Product Designer or similar UX related role for digital products
  • Excellent designing skills of page layouts and templates , passionate for quality.
  • Experienced with strong understanding in Front-End and UI Development.
  • Working knowledge of the following technologies and software: Adobe XD or Figma.
  • Solid experience in creating wireframes, storyboards, user flows, process flows and site maps
  • Strong time management skills, ability to manage multiple projects and deadlines including discernment of which projects are of greatest value to the company and marketing team.
  • Good grasp over color, typography, gradient, and other design elements
  • Conducting layout adjustments based on user feedback
  • Ability to convert concepts into design
  • Ability to offer various solutions
  • Basic knowledge in HTML, CSS will be PLUS
Job Responsibilities:
  • Come up with new design from scratch based on client requirements
  • Develop UI mockups to illustrate information design and functionality
  • Design UI with all elements for app(s), web control panel and website
  • Must follow style standards on fonts, colors and images
  • Conduct layout adjustments based on feedback
  • Ability to flourish with minimal guidance, be proactive, and handle uncertainty
  • Continually follow latest design trends and best practice standards
  • Stay up-to-date with the latest UI&UX trends, techniques, and technologies.
Educational Requirements:

Graduate from any reputed university.
